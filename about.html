Meet the Founders, your first friends.

We are a team of university students who are enthusiastic about technology. After learning how to solve problems through coding, our aim is to help other techies like us to learn really fast without the hustle of getting quality resources. All this is just for free, you won't have to sell your kidney! We are doing something like a Massive Online Open Course, but all for the love of tech! Trying to get every passionate learner on board with computer technology in the fastest and most fun way possible! Any assistance offered will be highly appreciated.
